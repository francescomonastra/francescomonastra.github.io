I am a PhD student in Astrophysics with a background in high-energy astrophysics instrumentation, detector simulations, and computational analysis for future space missions.

My current research interests include X-ray instrumentation, Transition-Edge Sensor detectors, Geant4 simulations, background modeling, and machine-learning techniques for event classification and background suppression.

I completed my Master's degree in Astronomy and Astrophysics at Sapienza University of Rome with a thesis titled 
<a href="/files/Tesi_Magistrale-FM.pdf" target="_blank"><em>Geant4 Simulations of Transition-Edge Sensors Detectors for the Large X-ray Observatory ATHENA and the Solar Axion Experiment IAXO</em></a>.
